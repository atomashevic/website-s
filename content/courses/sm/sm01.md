## Najvažnije formule


$$ z = \frac{\overline{X} - \mu_0}{\sigma _{\overline{X}}}$$


$$ \sigma _{\overline{X}} = \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}$$

$$ \overline{X} - z_{\frac{\alpha}{2}}\sigma_{\overline{X}} \leq \mu \leq   \overline{X} + z_{\frac{\alpha}{2}}\sigma_{\overline{X}}                     $$
