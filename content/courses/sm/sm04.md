## Najvažnije formule


$$z = \frac{p - \pi_o}{s_p}$$


{{% alert note %}}
$$ s_p = \sqrt{\frac{\pi_0 (1-\pi_0)}{n}}$$
{{% /alert %}}

$$z = \frac{p_1 - p_2}{s_{p_1-p_2}}$$

$$ \overline{p} = \frac{f_1 + f_2}{n_1+n_2}$$

{{% alert note %}}
$$ s_p = \sqrt{\overline{p}(1 - \overline{p})(\frac{1}{n_1}+\frac{1}{n_2})}$$
{{% /alert %}}
